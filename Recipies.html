Indian Recipes

Indian snacks are usually delicious and mouth watering. Savouries like samosas, aloo tikki, dhokla etc. are commonly served in India. This section is comprised of recipes which have been passed on for generation together in Indian homes. The crispness and tempting tastes of indian snacks is liked by young and old alike. These can be easily prepared at any time. It is ideal to serve snacks with coffee or tea.
